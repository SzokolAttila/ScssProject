Lightbound Boreus

This behemoth is the Radiant variant of the first Frost behemoth - Boreus. It shoots laser beams, spawns Radiant minions and deals a great charged area damage.

Behemoths

Lightbound Boreus

Is a part of the Boreus genus. Can be found on Conundrum Rocks.

Armor

Boreal Epiphany

The Helmet of the Boreus genus.

Boreal Resolve

The Breastplate of the Boreus genus.

Boreal Might

The Gauntlets of the Boreus genus.

Boreal March

The Greaves of the Boreus genus.

Weapons

Onus of Boreus

Using your special continually generates Frost Sprites that grant your next attack +30 damage and minor frost damage (Max 4 Sprites)

Turmoil of Boreus

After each charge attack level, generate 2 Frost Sprites that grant your next attack +30 damage and minor frost damage (Max 4 Sprites)

Brutality of Boreus

Storing or spending ammo continually generates Frost Sprites for 6 seconds that grant your next attack +30 damage and minor frost damage (Max 4 Sprites)

Revolution of Boreus

Periodically enter a rage, dealing +5% damage and gaining immunity to stagger. Taking damage causes you to enrage more quickly.

Destiny of Boreus

After using your Special, generate 3 Frost Sprites that grant your next attack +30 damage and minor frost damage (Max 4 Sprites)

Voice of Boreus

The last 4 shots in your magazine generates Frost Sprites that grant your next attack +30 damage and minor frost damage (Max 4 Sprites)

Brawlers of Boreus

After Surging, continually generate Frost Sprites for 6 seconds that grant your next attack +30 damage and minor frost damage (Max 4 Sprites)
