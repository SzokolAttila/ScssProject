Hellion

A mixture of a chicken and a lizard. It is the highest level normal behemoth in the game, which makes it especially good for farming experience. It deals damage with close combat and the sweeps of its tail.

Behemoths

Hellion

Is a part of the Hellion genus. Can be found on Sunderstone.

Is a part of the Hellion genus. Cannot be found on any of the islands.

Scorchstone Hellion (Heroic)

Is a part of the Hellion genus. Can be found on Twilight Sanctuary and The Blazeworks.
