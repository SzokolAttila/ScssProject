Skarn

This is the first Terra behemoth any Slayer will most likely encounter. It looks like a turtle, it has high health-stats and low damage. It can use its armor to further decrease damage taken.

Behemoths

Skarn

Is a part of the Skarn genus. Can be found on Iron Falls and Sunderstone.

Rockfall Skarn

Is a part of the Skarn genus. Cannot be found on any of the islands.

Rockfall Skarn (Heroic)

Is a part of the Skarn genus. Can be found on Hades Reach and The Paradox Breaks.
