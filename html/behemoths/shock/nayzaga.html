Nayzaga

This Shock type behemoth resembles something between the Quillshot and a crocodile. Being hit by its spikes can trigger the Shocked effect, which makes any Slayer unable to use consumables.

Behemoths

Nayzaga

Is a part of the Nayzaga genus. Can be found on Thudnerwatch and Brightwood.

Shockjaw Nayzaga

Is a part of the Nayzaga genus. Cannot be found on any of the islands.

Shockjaw Nayzaga (Heroic)

Is a part of the Nayzaga genus. Can be found on The Snowblind Waste
