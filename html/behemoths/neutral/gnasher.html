Gnasher

A beaver-like Neutral behemoth that uses it's tail and feet to stamp Slayers into the ground. The Ragetail version sends shockwaves after each slap with its tail.

Behemoths

Lesser Gnasher

Is a part of the Gnasher genus. Can be found on Boreal Outpost and Revelation Rock.

Gnasher

Is a part of the Gnasher genus. Can be found on Revelation Rock, Iron Falls and Aulric's Peak.

Ragetail Gnasher

Is a part of the Gnasher genus. Can be found on Aulric's Peak and Conundrum Rocks.

Ragetail Gnasher (Heroic)

Is a part of the Gnasher genus. Can be found on Coldrunner Key.
