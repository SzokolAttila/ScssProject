Swords

The sword has three main components: core combos, Valour combos, and the special meter. The core combos are the most basic combos, and they are the primary source of generating Valour. Once enough Valour is obtained, Valour combos become available, which consume the built-up Valour. Landing the final hit of combos will also increase the Special meter. At full meter, the special becomes available, which generates Valour and has unique properties.

Mods

Mending Bladecore

Each Valour gained or spent restores 30 health

Recursive Hilt

Dealing damage with Bladerush restores 20 stamina and generates 3 Valour over 9 seconds. Does not stack.

Charged Swordfocus

Instead of dealing damage over time, Resounding Echo deals damage once after it expires and deals +35% total damage.

Keen Edge

After dealing damage with the Bladesurge projectile, increase your movement speed by 25% for 10 seconds. Additionally, after the Bladesurge projectile travels a short distance, the damage is increased by 100%.

Dynamic Bladecore

After using any Valour combo, your next Valour combo within 5 seconds deals +30% damage.

Specials

Valiant Overdrive

Activate at full meter to enter Overdrive, gaining +20% attack speed and causing attacks to deal +60-80 aoe damage. Grants 1 Valour immediately and then 1 Valour every 4 seconds. Reactivate to dash in a direction (Costs 1 Valour).

Ardent Cyclone

Activate at full meter to spin, dealing 70 damage constantly to nearby enemies. Grants 1 Valour every 4 seconds. Reactivate to slash in a direction, dealing 500 + (10 x number of spin hits) to the first enemy hit.

Feast

Activate at full meter to enter the Feast, gaining the effects of Valiant Overdrive, +25% damage and +12% lifesteal. Health is constantly drained. Dashing also grants invulnerability

Avenging Overdrive

Activate at full meter to enter Overdrive, gaining +20% attacks speed and causing attacks to deal +60-80 aoe damage. Reactivate to parry and riposte a Behemoth attack, dealing 800 damage and granting 1 Valour when successful (2 second cooldown)

Legendaries

The Cauterizer
Blaze

Legendary Ability: enters a rage, gaining new attacks for 15 seconds. Deal +25% damage when the target is below 50% health. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Hypothermica
Frost

Legendary Ability: creates a Frost Shield that blocks hostile projectiles for 6 seconds. Damages and chills Behemoths that contact it (can interrupt). Resets on Behemoth kill or after 3.5 minutes.

Cyclonic Fury
Shock

Legendary Ability: teleports to a Behemoth, dealing shock damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.

Poisonous Thorn
Terra

Legendary Ability: revives all Slayers in a large radius, granting them healing over time and immunity to stagger. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 5 minutes.

Edge of Tomorrow
Radiant

Legendary Ability: rewinds to your position 3 seconds prior to reactivation. Restores health and stamina lost in that time, and removes debuffs. Become invulnerable and gain increased move speed for 6 seconds. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Voidbane
Umbral

Legendary Ability: Deals umbral damage to nearby enemies and teleport high into the sky. Then choose a spot to slam down on to, dealing additional umbral damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.
