Swords

The sword has three main components: core combos, Valour combos, and the special meter. The core combos are the most basic combos, and they are the primary source of generating Valour. Once enough Valour is obtained, Valour combos become available, which consume the built-up Valour. Landing the final hit of combos will also increase the Special meter. At full meter, the special becomes available, which generates Valour and has unique properties.

Mods

Mending Bladecore

Each Valour gained or spent restores 30 health

Recursive Hilt

Dealing damage with Bladerush restores 20 stamina and generates 3 Valour over 9 seconds. Does not stack.

Charged Swordfocus

Instead of dealing damage over time, Resounding Echo deals damage once after it expires and deals +50% total damage.

Keen Edge

After dealing damage with the Bladesurge projectile, increase your movement speed by 25% for 10 seconds. Additionally, after the Bladesurge projectile travels a short distance, the damage is increased by 100%.

Dynamic Bladecore

After using any Valour combo, your next Valour combo within 5 seconds deals +30% damage.
