Ostian Repeaters

A fully-ranged option for the fleet of foot. Dance the line between close and long range as you dish out damage, buffs, and traps. There is a fair amount of damage falloff as you move farther away from behemoths. The closer you are, the more damage you do.

Chambers

Full-Bore Chamber

Fires a piercing bolt in a line, dealing damage that falls as it travels. Empowered: Deals greatly increased damage and pushes the slayer backwards. Has a 20s cooldown.

Salvo Chamber

Fires an explosive missile in a line, dealing 450 damage in an area upon impact. Empowered: Fires 3 missiles in a spread. Has a 15s cooldown.

Marksman Chamber

Fires a shot in a line, dealing 250 damage to the first Behemoth hit. If a part is struck, that part takes 40% more damage from Shots for 10 seconds. Empowered: Damage bonus to increased to 80% and 25% to all allies. Has a 20s cooldown.

Grips

Captain's Grip

Launches a collectable buff a short distance, granting 15% increased attack speed for 15 seconds when collected. Empowered: Grants the same buff to all Slayers. Has a 20s cooldown.

Saboteur's Grip

Launches an explosive mine that deal 400 damage. Mine charged after a short period, increased damage to 700. Empowered: Deals 900 damage when charged and can interrupt Behemoths. Has a 25s cooldown.

Mods

Capacitive Magazine

Using an ability returns 8 ammo to the magazine.

Scoped Sights

Increases damage falloff range by 25%.

Lucky Magazine

Grants 2% critical strike chance for each round missing from the magazine.

Extraction Catalyst

Reduce both ability cooldowns by 2 seconds when dodging through Behemoth attacks.

Lightweight Frame

Gain 20% increased movespeed when at 6 or less ammo.

Precision Sights

Deal 5 increased damage when attacking the same part multiple times. Stacks up to 8 times.

Tactician's Magazine

Empowered Reloading at exactly 0 ammo decreases the cooldown of both abilities by 4 seconds.

Demolition Sights

While empowered, deal +100% damage to minions, fauna, and objects created by Behemoths.

Special - The Twin Suns

Barrel: While Empowered, gain +40% critical strike chance instead of bonus damage.

Chamber: Load 8 Mag Bombs into your magazine. They attach to enemies and are detonated on Reload. The first Mag Bomb attached to each Behemoth part deals +300 damage.

Grip: Dash in a direction, becoming invulnerable for the duration. After dodging through an attack, fire 6 mini-shots that always crit. While in Mag Bomb mode, fires 2 Mag Bombs instead.

Prism: After a critical strike, reduce the cooldown of Mag Bomb mode by 3 seconds.

Legendaries

Volcanic Eruptions
Blaze

Legendary Ability: enters a rage, gaining new attacks for 15 seconds. Deal +25% damage when the target is below 50% health. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Cyro Cannons
Frost

Legendary Ability: creates a Frost Shield that blocks hostile projectiles for 6 seconds. Damages and chills Behemoths that contact it (can interrupt). Resets on Behemoth kill or after 3.5 minutes.

Dynamic Deadlights
Shock

Legendary Ability: teleports to a Behemoth, dealing shock damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.

Tainted Needles
Terra

Legendary Ability: revives all Slayers in a large radius, granting them healing over time and immunity to stagger. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 5 minutes.

Temporal Mechanics
Radiant

Legendary Ability: rewinds to your position 3 seconds prior to reactivation. Restores health and stamina lost in that time, and removes debuffs. Become invulnerable and gain increased move speed for 6 seconds. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Eldritch Torments
Umbral

Legendary Ability: Deals umbral damage to nearby enemies and teleport high into the sky. Then choose a spot to slam down on to, dealing additional umbral damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.
