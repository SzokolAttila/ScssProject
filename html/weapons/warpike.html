Warpikes

A gradual build with a powerful payoff. Inflict tactical wounds to build up your meter, then pack that power into devastating mortar rounds. The more you store, the more they hurt.

Mods

Munitions Amplifier

Stored ammo quality increases slowly over time, up to max. Additionally, it buffs ammo quality by an extra +25%.

Lightweight Shaft

Pike charge attack no longer costs stamina and always critically strikes.

Balanced Spearhead

Increases damage dealt by 5% each time Piercing and Harvesting attacks are alternated. After 5 combo swaps, gain a +25% damage bonus for 8s. Does not stack. Resets upon combo end.

Barbed Spearhead

After a dodge piercing attack, gain +50% wound damage for 8 seconds

Executioners Spearhead

Each time the Behemoth is wounded, gain +8% damage for 45 seconds. Getting a wound refreshes the timer. Stacks up to 5.

Specials

Concussive Payload

Wellspring Press to convert meter into ammo. Hold to spend ammo to create an aura that buffs nearby allies with up to +30% critical strike chance based on ammo quality for 10 seconds

Savage Wellspring

Press to convert meter into ammo. Hold to spend ammo to create an aura that buffs nearby allies with up to +30% critical strike chance based on ammo quality for 10 seconds

Godhand

Use to convert meter into ammo. Hold to spend ammo to channel a beam that deals more damage to Behemoths the more it remains damaging them, up to 200%.

Reckless Leap

Press to convert meter into ammo. Hold to spend ammo and perform a flying lunge attack that deals up to 1500 damage based on distance and ammo quality

Legendaries

Pyroclastic Envoy
Blaze

Legendary Ability: enters a rage, gaining new attacks for 15 seconds. Deal +25% damage when the target is below 50% health. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Cold Hell
Frost

Legendary Ability: creates a Frost Shield that blocks hostile projectiles for 6 seconds. Damages and chills Behemoths that contact it (can interrupt). Resets on Behemoth kill or after 3.5 minutes.

The Conductor
Shock

Legendary Ability: teleports to a Behemoth, dealing shock damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.

Parasitic Curse
Terra

Legendary Ability: revives all Slayers in a large radius, granting them healing over time and immunity to stagger. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 5 minutes.

Millennium's End
Radiant

Legendary Ability: rewinds to your position 3 seconds prior to reactivation. Restores health and stamina lost in that time, and removes debuffs. Become invulnerable and gain increased move speed for 6 seconds. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

The Apocalypse Needle
Umbral

Legendary Ability: Deals umbral damage to nearby enemies and teleport high into the sky. Then choose a spot to slam down on to, dealing additional umbral damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.
