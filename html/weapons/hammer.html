Hammers

A lethal union of cudgel and cannon. Use the maul to splinter bones and scales, then finish things off with a blast from the cannon. You can even leverage explosions to dodge.

Mods

Weighted Crown

Stagger damage is no longer halved when attacking Behemoth legs.

Impulse Crown

Aerial Strike grants increased damage dealt for 10 seconds. Bonus is doubled if a Behemoth's head is struck.

Extended Clip

The Hammer's ammo is increased to 6.

Antique Clip

Ammo decreased to 2, but bonus from aethercharged ammo is increased by 300%

Turbine Trigger

Priming attacks increases attack speed by 10% until the combo ends. Stacks.

Specials

Concussive Salvo

Hold to load ammo into the hammer. Release to unleash a blast that deals up to 360 damage based on ammo loaded and can interrupt behemoths. Range increases with ammo loaded.

Mighty Landbreaker

Hold to load ammo into the hammer. Release to create an aether vent that buffs players who step over it, granting up to +35% Damage. Additionally gives +25% movementspeed for 8 sec.

Molten Edict

Converts all explosive uses of ammo into fireball attacks that deal damage based on ammo quality.

Ironheart Landbreaker

Hold to load ammo into the hammer. Release to create an aether vent that buffs players who step over it, granting them +40% damage reduction and up to 155 healing over 30 seconds when aethercharged.

Legendaries

Magma Quake
Blaze

Legendary Ability: enters a rage, gaining new attacks for 15 seconds. Deal +25% damage when the target is below 50% health. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Bomb Cyclone
Frost

Legendary Ability: creates a Frost Shield that blocks hostile projectiles for 6 seconds. Damages and chills Behemoths that contact it (can interrupt). Resets on Behemoth kill or after 3.5 minutes.

Galvanic Impact
Shock

Legendary Ability: teleports to a Behemoth, dealing shock damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.

Unsteady Ground
Terra

Legendary Ability: revives all Slayers in a large radius, granting them healing over time and immunity to stagger. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 5 minutes.

History's Burden
Radiant

Legendary Ability: rewinds to your position 3 seconds prior to reactivation. Restores health and stamina lost in that time, and removes debuffs. Become invulnerable and gain increased move speed for 6 seconds. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Sanity Check
Umbral

Legendary Ability: Deals umbral damage to nearby enemies and teleport high into the sky. Then choose a spot to slam down on to, dealing additional umbral damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.
