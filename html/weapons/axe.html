Axes

A heavy hitter for tactical Slayers. The Axe is a relatively slow hitting weapon, making up for it with substantial damage on each hit. To make the most of an Axe a Slayer must learn to make the most of each opening, committing time to empowering attacks when they can. With proper use of this ability to invest in charging attacks a Slayer is rewarded by an escalating damage buff and even the ability to stand firm through Behemoth attacks. Top this off with some of the most powerful special attacks available, enhanced by proper management of charging attacks and determination, and the Axe gives a tactically minded Slayer a devastating weapon against the Behemoths.

Mods

Volatile Axecore

Charging attack deals aoe damage to nearby enemies at each charge level.

Lightweight Haft

Movement speed while charging Horizontal Attacks is now increased instead of decreased and doesn't cost stamina to maintain.

Overcharged Cylinder

Determination can now stack up to 4 times.

Railsplitter's Canteen

Tonics consumed while your axe is thrown have +50% effectiveness

Furious Axecore

Perfectly charged attacks grants +15 meter on hit, but no longer grant resolve.

Specials

Flight of Ruin

Throw the axe, dealing 60 damage constantly and gaining 2 meter per hit, reactivate to call it back and deal a powerful attack that deals up to 500 damage (further increased by determination) and gain 15 meter.

Grim Onslaught

Hurl the axe forward, dealing up to 800 damage (further increased by determination) to enemies in its path and gaining 15 meter when hitting a Behemoth. Catch the axe to gain 30 meter.

Relentless Onslaught

Hurl the axe forward, damaging enemies in its path. Catch the axe to throw it again up to 2 more times in the next 5 seconds, dealing 500/600/1000 damage (further increased by determination). Does not build meter.

Legendaries

Incinerator's Song
Blaze

Legendary Ability: enters a rage, gaining new attacks for 15 seconds. Deal +25% damage when the target is below 50% health. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Permanent Frost
Frost

Legendary Ability: creates a Frost Shield that blocks hostile projectiles for 6 seconds. Damages and chills Behemoths that contact it (can interrupt). Resets on Behemoth kill or after 3.5 minutes.

Terminal Voltage
Shock

Legendary Ability: teleports to a Behemoth, dealing shock damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.

Malignant Scourge
Terra

Legendary Ability: revives all Slayers in a large radius, granting them healing over time and immunity to stagger. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 5 minutes.

Existential Crisis
Radiant

Legendary Ability: rewinds to your position 3 seconds prior to reactivation. Restores health and stamina lost in that time, and removes debuffs. Become invulnerable and gain increased move speed for 6 seconds. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Mindsplitter
Umbral

Legendary Ability: Deals umbral damage to nearby enemies and teleport high into the sky. Then choose a spot to slam down on to, dealing additional umbral damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.
