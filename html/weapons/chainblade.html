Chain Blades

Fast hitting dual blades with S+ mobility. Swing the blades from their chains to slash from a distance, or pull yourself in for an up-close assault.

Mods

Lightweight Chain

Increases the lenght of your chains by +25%. Your Chain Pull deals +25% damage and generates an additional 20 meter.

Hurricane Blades

Using Blade Spin grants stacking on-next hit damage that is consumed when using other attacks.

Serrated Blades

Bladed attacks have a chance to cause your next 4 lacerating attacks to deal +50% wound damage.

Momentum Blades

After using a special, your next special use within 4 seconds consumes no momentum

Demolition Blades

Deal +50% damage to minions, fauna and objects created by Behemoths. On killing a minion, fauna, or object, gain 1 momentum.

Specials

Reaper's Dance

Push off a nearby target with invulnerability, then use primary or secondary attacks to perform a followup. Primary: A dash attack to the target that consumes 1 momentum. Secondary: A spinning slam that consumes all momentum and deals greatly increased damage per momentum consumed. (Consume 1 momentum if no follow up is performed.)

Cruel Riftstrike

Teleport forward with invulnerability and strike the nearest Behemoth part, dealing 75 damage and applying a mark that deals 25 damage per second for 7 seconds. When a mark expires it deals 200 damage for each other mark active at the time. Mark duration increeased to 10 seconds if an attack is avoided during the teleport.

Insatible Dance

Push off a nearby target with invulnerability, then use primary or secondary attacks to perform a followup. Primary: A dash attack to the target that consumes 1 momentum. Secondary: A spinning slam that consumes all momentum to heal you and create a 150 healing orb for each momentum consumed. Targets at or healed to full health gain 5% damage for 10 seconds, stacking up to 4 times. (Consume 1 momentum if no follow up is performed.)

Legendaries

Scorching Agonies
Blaze

Legendary Ability: enters a rage, gaining new attacks for 15 seconds. Deal +25% damage when the target is below 50% health. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Blizzard's Teeth
Frost

Legendary Ability: creates a Frost Shield that blocks hostile projectiles for 6 seconds. Damages and chills Behemoths that contact it (can interrupt). Resets on Behemoth kill or after 3.5 minutes.

Electric Cruelties
Shock

Legendary Ability: teleports to a Behemoth, dealing shock damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.

Death Blossoms
Terra

Legendary Ability: revives all Slayers in a large radius, granting them healing over time and immunity to stagger. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 5 minutes.

The Weeping Hours
Radiant

Legendary Ability: rewinds to your position 3 seconds prior to reactivation. Restores health and stamina lost in that time, and removes debuffs. Become invulnerable and gain increased move speed for 6 seconds. Usable while downed. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 90 seconds.

Night Terrors
Umbral

Legendary Ability: Deals umbral damage to nearby enemies and teleport high into the sky. Then choose a spot to slam down on to, dealing additional umbral damage. Resets on Behemoth kill or after 2 minutes.
